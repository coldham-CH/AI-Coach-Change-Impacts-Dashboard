Learning transformation context Tab 1

North Star. The transformation moves Cisco from a legacy, role-based, fragmented learning environment to a skills-first, AI-augmented, practice-oriented, operationalized learning culture.

Key operational shift. Learning is no longer an “extra” task; it is integrated into the workflow (Time to Grow), supported by AI (BetterUp), practiced in simulations (Mursion), and validated by data (Skills Studio).

Implementation focus. The primary challenge across areas is change saturation. Success relies on leaders modeling an inquiry-based culture, protecting time for growth, and encouraging safe, repetitive practice through simulations.

Key implementation considerations (Tab 2)

  • Executive advocacy: Executive leaders must reinforce that the AI Coach is a “Private Sandbox” for development, not a reporting tool for HR or management.
  • Work Council countries: Ensure the Whole Person Assessment and 360 Feedback is disabled in regions where it conflicts with local Works Council regulations.
  • Sales specifics: Sales teams already use multiple specialized coaches (e.g., MindTickle); messaging must differentiate BetterUp AI Coach (leadership development vs. deal-cycle coaching).
  • Guardrails & escalation: Guardrails will be in place to direct users to the Crisis team or Employee Relations when human support is needed, based on defined criteria (topics the AI Coach must not handle).
  • Support partners: People Partners and Leader Consultants should guide employees on when to use the AI Coach versus human support.
  • Support & troubleshooting: Technical issues with BetterUp or data sync follow a dedicated support path, distinct from general CircuIT support (placeholder for link/process in source workbook).

Universal narrative

What applies to everyone

Systems, tools & data

BetterUp integrates with Cisco starting with CircuIT as the front door and Workday for employee data (future: Skills Studio, Webex performance flows, Outlook, Onboarding, Leading Edge, Robin). BetterUp AI Coach lives in BetterUp (external confidential space). Handoff from CircuIT is disconnected—users re-prompt in BetterUp; new conversation each login. Optional Whole Person Assessment as first onboarding step, retakable. Features include voice role-play, core values, gratitude, scenario simulator (accessibility), insights reporting, Discover library, Story+Coaching, self-assessment library; future 360. LEAD human coaching shares one platform but certain LEAD features sunset after six months; human and AI coaching threads do not cross-feed. Workday profile data personalizes experience; aggregate reporting on Guiding Principles and Leadership Expectations from topics and Whole Person data.

Processes & policies

Inquiry-first coaching; Whole Person Assessment shares growth insights on the GPs and LEs; strict guardrails—no hiring/firing/comp/PMER/ER topics; escalation paths to ER, Crisis, Peeps, Leader Consultants.

Shared risks

Fragmented experience (CircuIT → BetterUp, reprompt, no history carryover; human vs AI silos). Oversaturation of AI tools; Sales has multiple “coaches.” Perception risk (HR coach, tool-of-month, ChatGPT substitute). Coaching clarity (human vs AI; inquiry vs answers). Whole Person Assessment length / progress visibility; need to clarify it’s optional.

Shared benefits

24/7 accessibility including mobile; credible coach (ICF + behavior science + Cisco context); personalization from Workday; versatile whole-life + professional support; capability progress signal when Whole Person Assessment is used; supplement—not replace—human coaching (RSS / LEAD / Executive coaching per audience).

Most compelling benefit by stakeholder group Leader Check-In messaging

One headline benefit each, aligned to Leader Check-In_BetterUp AI Coach High-level Messaging.docx (not repeating the full benefit stack above).

Team Members (ICs)

Clarity in the moment. A private space to think through challenges before they escalate, prepare for important conversations, and build confidence—available 24/7 with guidance tailored to Cisco context, grounded in behavioral science rather than generic advice.

People Leaders

A partner for the moments you often carry alone. Pressure-test decisions, navigate team and sensitive situations, and get unstuck without stacking more meetings—while strengthening how you coach others and focus on highest-impact work, including living Leadership Expectations (coaching teams to their best, delivering with quality and urgency, leading change and embracing AI). Especially fills a gap for leaders without regular People Partner access.

Support (PeePs, LCs, People Support)

Human support used more effectively. Positioning matches the enterprise story: the coach is not a replacement—it helps employees use leaders, coaches, and People Partners more effectively after reflection in a confidential space.

Executives

Scale leadership capability without replacing human coaching. Extends high-quality coaching across the business, reinforces how leaders think and decide at every level, aligns with Leadership Expectations, and complements existing human coaching with always-on access between sessions—supporting faster, more confident decisions across teams.
